How to Use the Foam Cannon Wash Kit

Using the Foam Cannon Wash Kit is simple and straightforward:

  1. Fill the foam cannon with the provided car wash soap and water mixture.
  2. Attach the foam cannon to your pressure washer.
  3. Spray the foam evenly over your car, covering all surfaces.
  4. Let the foam sit for a few minutes to loosen dirt and grime.
  5. Rinse off the foam with clean water.
  6. Dry your car with a microfiber towel for a spotless finish.
